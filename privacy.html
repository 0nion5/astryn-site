Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Your privacy is fundamental to Astryn. We collect only what's necessary and never sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Astryn, we collect: • Account Information: Email address and name when you sign up • Birth Details: Date, time, and place of birth to calculate your natal chart • Journal Entries: The content you write in your journal • Chat Conversations: Messages exchanged with the AI companion • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our service • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: • Calculate your birth chart and provide personalised cosmic guidance • Generate AI-powered insights, reflections, and forecasts • Improve and develop new features • Send you notifications you've opted into • Ensure the security and integrity of our service • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI Processing

Your birth details, journal entries, and conversations are processed by AI systems to generate insights. This processing: • Uses third-party AI services with enterprise-grade security • Does not use your data to train AI models • Is essential to provide the core functionality of the app

4. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously: • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256) • We use secure cloud infrastructure with industry-standard protections • Access to user data is strictly limited and logged • Regular security updates are performed

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with: • Service Providers: Essential services like hosting, AI processing, and payment processing (Apple) • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and export your data, to object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, use the Settings page in the app or contact us at hello@elumahq.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, personal data is deleted within 30 days, anonymised analytics may be retained, and backup deletion occurs within 90 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Astryn is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. When this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK ICO.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us
